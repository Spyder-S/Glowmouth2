What Is GlowMouth

Imaging, analysis, and dentist guidance — at home.

GlowMouth is an at-home oral health monitoring system. It pairs a handheld QLF imaging device with AI analysis and app-based tracking to surface early indicators of oral health changes between dental visits.

Intraoral imaging device

Uses QLF technology at 405nm to capture five standard angles inside your mouth in under two minutes.

Clinically-informed AI analysis

Computer vision models detect surface patterns associated with plaque, gum tissue changes, and enamel wear.

Daily GlowScore

A single 1–100 number summarizing your oral health indicators for the day, tracked over time in the app.

Who It's For

For anyone who wants more visibility between dental appointments.

Whether you're managing a condition that affects oral health, building better habits, or want earlier awareness of changes — GlowMouth gives you data your toothbrush can't.

How It Works

From scan to insight in three steps.

Designed to fit into your existing oral care routine. No clinic visit required.

STEP 01

Scan

Capture five standard angles inside your mouth in under two minutes using the GlowMouth device. The app guides every position.

STEP 02

Analyze

A computer vision model examines each frame for surface patterns across three indicator categories: plaque, gum tissue, and enamel.

STEP 03

Track

Review your daily GlowScore, see which regions flagged elevated signals, and monitor your trend over days and weeks.
