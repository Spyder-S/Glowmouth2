How It Works

From scan to insight in under two minutes.

GlowMouth's workflow is designed to fit into your existing oral care routine — quick, consistent, and informative.

01

Scan with the QLF device

The GlowMouth device is a compact handheld intraoral camera. Brush as you normally would, then follow the app's guided overlays to capture five standard angles — front, left side, right side, upper arch, and lower arch. The full capture takes under two minutes.

The app performs real-time quality checks, detecting blur and poor lighting frame-by-frame. If a frame doesn't meet the threshold, the app prompts a retake before anything uploads.

02

AI processes the fluorescence data

Your images upload encrypted (TLS 1.3) to GlowMouth's analysis pipeline. A computer vision model trained on QLF intraoral images examines each frame for surface patterns — surfacing signals for plaque-like film, gum tissue changes, and enamel surface characteristics. Analysis typically completes in 30–45 seconds.

Per-frame scores are aggregated across all five angles, so no single low-quality frame skews your result. Every result carries a confidence level: High, Medium, or Low.

03

Review your GlowScore, stored and tracked over time

Your GlowScore (1–100) summarizes the AI's assessment across all three indicator categories. The app shows you an oral health map — color-coded by region — identifying which areas registered elevated signals and which indicator is driving the change.

The score is stored in your account. You can view your 7-day, 30-day, and 90-day trend at any time. A consistent upward trend as you adjust your routine is the signal to watch, not any single day's number.

04

Share results with your dentist (optional)

Every scan generates a structured summary — GlowScore, per-indicator readings, confidence level, scan date — exportable as a PDF or shareable via the in-app partner portal. GlowMouth integrates with dental practices in our partner network.

Sharing is always optional. GlowMouth provides additional longitudinal data that can supplement what your dentist observes at a twice-yearly visit. It does not replace clinical assessment.

What GlowMouth measures

Three indicator categories are scored per scan. Each is weighted by severity of detected signal.

Plaque-like film
Gum tissue health
Enamel surface

What GlowMouth does not measure

Cavities — requires X-ray for confirmation
Bone density or bone loss
Cancer screening of any kind
Gum pocket depth — requires probing
Tooth alignment or bite issues
